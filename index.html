Jan 2015 – Mar 2016

Tribological Evaluation of Grease

Indian Institute of Science

This was my Capstone Project for my Bachelor's degree. Aim of this project was to modify prepared Vegetable-oil based grease to be used as eco-friendly substitute so that its physical properties comes close to Industrial/Petroleum-oil based greases. This was achieved by comparing conducting tests such as Cone Penetration Test, Roll Stability Test and 4 Ball Wear Scar Diameter test on both Vegetable Oil and Petroleum Oil based greases.
Conclusion was made that additives are required for Vegetable oil based greases to function on temperatures higher that 70℃ and economic ways needs to be found to mass produce Vegetable Oil based greases.
